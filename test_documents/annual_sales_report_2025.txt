# AIFlow Corp - Annual Sales & Financial Report (FY 2025)

## Executive Summary
For the fiscal year 2025 (FY2025), AIFlow Corp achieved a total gross revenue of $14,850,000, representing a 22.4% year-over-year increase compared to FY2024 ($12,130,000). Net profit margin reached 18.5%, yielding a net income of $2,747,250.

---

## Quarterly Revenue Breakdown
* **Q1 2025**: $3,120,000 (driven by winter software subscriptions)
* **Q2 2025**: $3,450,000 (lifted by enterprise renewals)
* **Q3 2025**: $3,880,000 (bolstered by new consulting service cohorts)
* **Q4 2025**: $4,400,000 (record-breaking holiday enterprise contracts)

---

## Regional Sales Performance
* **North America (NA)**: $6,830,000 (46% of total revenue)
* **Europe, Middle East, and Africa (EMEA)**: $4,158,000 (28% of total revenue)
* **Asia-Pacific (APAC)**: $2,673,000 (18% of total revenue)
* **Latin America (LATAM)**: $1,189,000 (8% of total revenue)

---

## Product Categories & Performance
1. **AIFlow Orchestration Engine (Core)**:
   * Revenue: $8,910,000
   * License count: 450 enterprise accounts
   * Average contract value (ACV): $19,800
2. **Consulting & Custom Integrations**:
   * Revenue: $3,564,000
   * Projects completed: 72 engagements
   * Average project price: $49,500
3. **Training & Support Subscriptions**:
   * Revenue: $2,376,000
   * Renewal rate: 89.2%

---

## Strategic Growth Areas
* **AI Security Modules**: In Q4, we launched a beta version of our compliance auditing framework which brought in $450,000 in early access fees (included in Core revenue).
* **Cloud Infrastructure Optimization**: Cloud migration reduced operating costs from $1,850,000 in FY2024 to $1,420,000 in FY2025.
